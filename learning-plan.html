Collaboration

When I work with others, I will ensure that:

  • I am respecting others' space and boundaries
  • If I need help, I will ask for help through a channel which allows people who are available and happy to assist me to do so
  • If I feel that I can offer helpful support to someone in need, I will offer it - I won't be offended if they decide that they don't want my help
  • I maintain a flexible mindset
  • I follow through with my word
  • I do not share information that has been provided to me in confidence, unless it is something that would cause harm to a person if not shared
  • I practice active listening
  • I ask insightful and helpful questions where appropriate
  • I do not gossip or speak ill of others
  • If I witness bad behavior, I stand up to it with non-violent communication
  • I treat others with respect and act with compassion

Skills to Develop

As an introverted cancerian, it can take me a while to feel comfortable around new people, but I don't like hanging out in my shell all the time. I find that my interactions are better with people when I am playful and open, rather than quiet and shy. I hope that this career pathway increases my self-confidence and allows me to truly be myself at all times.

I would also like to develop my leadership skills. I have been around a lot of strong leaders and feel that I have some of the skills required to become a good leader, but again, I need to increase my confidence in this area.

I would love to get a better handle on my time management skills. I have developed some perfectionistic tendencies through my prior study and work experience. This has served me well in circumstances where attention to detail is critical, but I also feel that perfectionism can get in the way of moving forward. Done is better than perfect. Sometimes wanting perfection prevents me from getting things done in a timely fashion, which increases my stress levels.

It would be amazing to know how to work with difficult personalities productively and in a way that doesn't affect me emotionally.

Responsible Back Up Plan

If things start to build up on me, I will:

  • Step back and take a moment to reflect
  • Assess my list, set obtainable goals, and re-prioritise as needed
  • Communicate with my facilitator in a timely fashion
  • Inform project collaborators in a timely fashion

If I get stuck, I will:

  • Seek help from my classmates and/or facilitator via Slack if I get stuck for more than 20 minutes
